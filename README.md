# paperclip

I built this to explore how two people can run a robot delivery company by "hiring" nine AI agents. Paperclip got me started in about 30 minutes—with a few hiccups, but overall smooth. The real challenge is finding a design pattern that makes this genuinely useful. I'd love to learn from your experiences, ideas, or struggles. Let's figure this out together.
